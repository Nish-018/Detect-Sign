Detect Sign is an innovative and inclusive communication application designed to bridge the gap between people with hearing disabilities and the rest of society. 
Communication is one of the most important parts of human life, yet millions of deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals face difficulties expressing themselves in everyday situations. 
It aims to solve this challenge by using modern technology to translate sign language into text and emojis in real time, making conversations easier, faster, and more expressive. 
The app uses advanced gesture recognition and artificial intelligence to identify hand signs and convert them into understandable messages instantly. Users can simply perform sign language gestures
in front of a camera, and the application translates them into written text along with suitable emojis that help express emotions and tone. 
This makes communication more interactive and natural, especially in schools, workplaces, hospitals, public places, and social environments. 
It is not only a translation tool but also a step toward creating a more accessible and inclusive world. It helps hearing individuals understand sign language users without needing prior knowledge of sign language.
By reducing communication barriers, the app promotes confidence, independence, and equal opportunities for people with hearing impairments.
